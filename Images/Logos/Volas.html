BRAND DESIGNER

Creating awareness
& treatment for cancer

Young Troupers is a Non-Governmental Organization creating a different approach in Cancer awareness and treatment. Their organization had not enough visibility and identity, and I was approached to provide visuals
and brand identity, as public awareness starts with in fact brand awareness.

STRATEGY

Defining a new chapter for
Young Troupers

I worked closely with the Young Troupers executives to position them strategically and develop their visual and brand identity. The branding process started with a deep dive with some research about the industry and audience.
The brand identity was built around purpose: using bright colors for youth and the symbols of cancer for connectivity

VISUAL IDENTITY

Young Troupers logo

I developed a visual identity that reflects Ace Potter's purpose, simplicity, excellence, transparency, and vibrancy, as well as a visual system.
